## Q&A

11. What is HBM?

    -   High-Bandwidth Memory. It is the memory technology used on most compute GPUs. Both the AMD MI100/200 series as the main compute GPUs of NVIDIA (P100, V100, A100, H100) and Intel (Ponte Vecchio) use it.
