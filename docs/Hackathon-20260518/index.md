## Preparation: Lectures about HPE and AMD profiling tools

As proper optimisation of code starts with understanding where the bottlenecks are, some understanding of
profiling and profiling tools is very useful. Therefore we highly recommend hackathon participants to follow
some lectures of the ["Performance Analysis and Optimization Workshop](../paow-20260511/index.md) that we 
have the week before.

It will be possible to follow the recommended lectures by Zoom so there is no need to actually join the
workshop on-site.

Recommended lectures:

-   Monday May 11, 11:20: [Introduction to Performance Analysis with Perftools](../paow-20260511/schedule.md#hackathon_1)

-   Monday May 11, 13:00: [Advanced Performance Analysis](../paow-20260511/schedule.md#hackathon_2)

    The lecture is followed by a half hour exercise session.

-   Tuesday May 12, 13:00: [AMD Profiling Tools and GPU optimisations 1](../paow-20260511/schedule.md#hackathon_3)

    The lecture is followed by a half hour exercise session.

-   Tuesday May 12, 14:50: [AMD Profiling Tools and GPU optimisations 2](../paow-20260511/schedule.md#hackathon_4)

    The lecture is followed by a half hour exercise session.

See also the [tentative schedule of the hackathon](schedule.md)
