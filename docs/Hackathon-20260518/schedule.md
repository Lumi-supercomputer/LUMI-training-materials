    -   13:30: Welcome and information

    -   15:30: Coffee break

    -   Until 17:30: Group work

-   Tuesday till Thursday, May 19-15

    -   9:00: Status update (max 5min each group)
        -   What did you work on yesterday?

        -   What issues are blocking you?

    -   9:15–12:00: Group work

    -   12:00–13:00: Lunch break. A sandwich lunch will be provided

    -   13:00–17:30: Group work

    -   15:00: Coffee break

-   Friday May 22

    -   9:00: Last group work

    -   10:00-12:00: Final presentations

        -   Which of your goals did you accomplish?
        -   What is left to do?
        -   What was the most important change implemented during this week?
        -   (How) did your performance improve?
  
    -   10:45: Coffee break

    -   12:00: End. A sandwich lunch will be offered. The room must be fully vacated by 14:00.
